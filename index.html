About Me

I’m Elizabeth Thomas, a cost analyst specialising in technology spend, forecasting, and strategic insight. I build intuitive, data-driven dashboards and models that help teams make better decisions.

I live in a small village in Lancashire with my cat, Kafka. I work remotely and spend most of my free time indoors, which suits me perfectly. I enjoy reading, playing video games, and knitting — anything that lets me unwind and focus on something thoughtful or creative.

In my work, I care deeply about clarity, accuracy, and purpose. I’m detail-driven and enjoy untangling complex information to make it simpler, cleaner, and more usable. I believe good analysis shouldn’t just look impressive — it should lead to better outcomes, whether that’s through stronger forecasts, smarter decisions, or more confident planning.
